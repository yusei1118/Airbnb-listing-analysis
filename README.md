 # Airbnb Pricing & Revenue Analysis


## Overview
This project analyzes Airbnb listing prices and revenue trends using Tableau and Python.

The goal is to understand how pricing varies across different locations and property characteristics, and how revenue changes over time.

---

## Data Preprocessing
Data preprocessing was performed using Python (Pandas) to clean missing values and prepare datasets for Tableau.

---

## Dashboard Overview

### 1. Average Price by Number of Bedrooms
- Listings with more bedrooms generally have higher average prices
- Prices increase significantly after 3+ bedrooms
- Small properties (0–1 bedrooms) are relatively cheap and stable in pricing

**Key Insight:**
Larger properties significantly increase average listing prices.

---

### 2. Revenue Trend Over Time (2016)
- Revenue shows a strong upward trend throughout the year
- Rapid growth in the first half of the year
- Slight stabilization in mid-year, followed by another increase toward the end of the year

**Key Insight:**
Seasonality and demand growth strongly impact revenue performance.

---

### 3. Price Distribution by Zipcode
- Clear variation in average prices across different zip codes
- Certain areas consistently show higher pricing clusters
- Lower-priced regions are also clearly grouped geographically

**Key Insight:**
Location is a major driver of Airbnb pricing differences.

---

### 4. Geographic Price Distribution
- Map visualization shows spatial clustering of high and low-priced areas
- High-value listings are concentrated in specific regions
- Some zones show mixed pricing, indicating diverse property types

---

## Key Takeaways
- Location is the strongest factor affecting Airbnb pricing
- Larger properties significantly increase listing prices
- Revenue shows an overall upward trend over time
- Pricing varies significantly across geographical regions
